About us

our history

LINK is a student activity that stands for the link between the student and actual work field, it was launched for the first time in 2017 at Cairo University By Kamal Nour Elden

our achievements

Since 2017, we have been able to develop the skills of students from various universities, and we have trained more than 1,000 students, and every season we have our own conference in order to present everything we learned during the sessions.
